Video Game Global Earnings by Year

What observations can we make from this line graph?

  • Global video game sales remained relatively calm until 1996, where sales broke through the $100M mark and even the $200M mark the following year. This matches the data found in the previous graph, as not only do the amount of video games increase by the decade, but also the technology used for making the games.

  • From 1996, yearly video game global sales continue to gradually increase until hitting a peak in 2008, earning a total of $678,900,000 that year. Following that, the yearly global earnings start to decline, hitting a bottom in 2016 with only $70,930,000 earned (partly due to the fact that the dataset isn't too populated with games from 2016 and beyond).

  • This data matches the findings in the previous slide, with total earnings staying relatively calm in the 1980s, slowly ramping up in the 1990s, hitting a peak in the 2000s, and slowly declining in the 2010s.
