Video Game Earnings by Platform

What observations can we make from this bar graph? (Global sales only)

  • When viewing video game data strictly through the platform, we can see that the crowd favorite is the Playstation 2 (PS2), with the second highest video game count of 2,127 and the highest global earnings with a total of $1,233,460,000.

  • Most consoles like the Playstation 2 (PS2), Xbox 360 (X360), and the Wii have a a high video game count and total earnings. Some exceptions are the WiiU with a low video game count and low total earnings, as well as the Playstation 4 (PS4) and Xbox One (XOne) due to the database only having games up to 2016.

  • Most handheld consoles also have pale in comparison to the consoles regarding overall earning success, with the exception of the Nintendo DS (DS) which has the highest video game count of 2,133 and the fifth highest total earnings of $818,960,000, outranking the Playstation (PS) but being under the Playstation 2 (PS2), Xbox 360 (X360), Wii and Playstation 3 (PS3).

  • The top three platforms based on total global earnings are the Playstation 2 ($1,233,460,000), Xbox 360 ($969,610,000), and Playstation 3 ($949,350,000). in that order. The top three platforms based on game count are the Nintendo DS (2,133), Playstation 2 (2,127), and Playstation 3 (1,304) in that order.
