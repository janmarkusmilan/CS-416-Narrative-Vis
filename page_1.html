Count of Video Games by Decade

What observations can we make from this line graph?

  • As the decades move on, the number of video games produced also increases. The sudden dip in the 2010s decade has to do with the fact that the dataset used only contains video games up to 2016. The real value for the decade should be near or above the 2000s decade.

  • Interestingly enough, the average global earnings seem to be decreasing by decade. This could be because not all video games perform well, especially considering the increasing amount of video games produced per decade.

  • Keep in mind that the dataset used is not completely all-inclusive. The dataset does not include the mobile platform, and a majority of the video games listed follow a buy-to-play business model. Some highest-grossing video games like Dungeon Fighter Online (2005) are not included as it is free-to-play.
